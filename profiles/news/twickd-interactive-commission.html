Twickd Interactive Commission

Published by Hugo
Oct 8th, 2019 - 04:09PM

Our vision is and has always been, to value hard work, integrity, loyalty and pride over profits. Twickd Interactive Commission is the best representation of this mindset. We want the developers who are already well rewarded for their efforts by the community to be even more rewarded with lower fees.

Benefits for Developers

Twickd Interactive Commission expand the already long list of benefits of becoming a Developer on Twickd.

  • The easiest registration process. You can become a developer on Twickd in less than 5 minutes!
  • Clear, yet precise Dashboard.
  • Packages going through moderation in less than 24 hours.
  • Package update going live in less than an hour, 24h/24, 7 days/week, 365 days/year. So your users are always up to date!
  • Get paid EVERY DAY! Read more about payouts in our Developer Policy
  • No more high fees from PayPal. We negotiated micropayments fees for every transaction on Twickd so you can earn more than you use to. Learn more
  • Lowest fees on the market! As low as 5%

And much more!

With the introduction of the Twickd Interactive Commission, we also introduced a brand new ranking system for developers! This system, based on your monthly performances, will give you access to higher ranks. The more you sell, the more you earn! That's the spirit.

To respect your privacy as a Developer, neither your customers and fellow Developers will know your rank.

We refresh theses ranks at the end of each month. If you achieved 100% or more of your monthly goal, you will be awarded with lowers fees for all of your new sales! For more information, please see our Developer Policy

Here is a, not limited, table of the Twickd Interactive Commission Tiers:

Tier Commission (%) Minimum Monthly Sales ($) Maximum Monthly Sales ($)
Tier 4 10 0 999
Tier 3 9 1000 2499.99
Tier 2 7 2500 4999.99
Tier 1 5 5000 9999.99

More information about the Interactive Commission Tier in our Developer Policy

If you have any question regarding this new feature, feel free to Contact us Thank you for using Twickd
